Iron Man

Iron Man is a superhero from Marvel Comics. His real name is Tony Stark. He is a genius billionaire and founding member of Avengers.

Origin Story

Tony Stark was a wealthy weapons manufacturer. He was kidnapped by terrorists in Afghanistan. He built his first iron suit to escape captivity.

Powers and Abilities

  1. Genius Level Intellect
  2. Advanced Technology Expert
  3. Powerful Armored Suits
  4. Flight Capability
  5. Repulsor Beams
  6. Super Strength in Suit

His Iconic Suit

Iron Man wears a high-tech armored suit. The suit is powered by an Arc Reactor. He has created over 50 different suit versions.

His assistant and love interest is Pepper Potts. His AI assistant is named J.A.R.V.I.S. later replaced by F.R.I.D.A.Y.. Tony Stark sacrificed his life to defeat Thanos in Endgame. He is known for his famous dialogue "I am Iron Man".
