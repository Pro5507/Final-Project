Thor

Thor is a superhero from Marvel Comics. He is the God of Thunder. He is the prince of Asgard and member of Avengers.

Origin Story

Thor is the son of Odin, the king of Asgard. He was banished to Earth for his arrogance. He learned humility and became worthy of his powers.

Powers and Abilities

  1. God-like Strength
  2. Control Over Thunder and Lightning
  3. Near Immortality
  4. Flight Ability
  5. Super Speed
  6. Enhanced Durability

His Iconic Weapons

Thor carries a magical hammer called Mjolnir. Only worthy people can lift this powerful hammer. Later he got a new weapon called Stormbreaker. Stormbreaker is a powerful axe made in Nidavellir.

His brother Loki is the God of Mischief. Thor's home planet Asgard was destroyed in Ragnarok. His love interest is Jane Foster who later becomes Mighty Thor. He is one of the most powerful Avengers in the team.
